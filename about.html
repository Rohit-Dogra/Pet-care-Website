  2. Use the same commands and rewards to avoid confusion.

  4. Be patient and consistent, as dogs learn at their own pace.

  1. Set regular feeding, walking, and playtime schedules for stability.
  2. Use toys, puzzles, and training to keep your pet engaged.
  3. Keep your home and pet areas tidy to promote health and safety.
  4. Schedule routine vet visits to monitor your pet’s health.

  1. Brush your pet’s coat regularly to remove tangles, dirt, and loose hair.
  2. Use pet-friendly shampoos and bathe your pet as needed to keep their coat clean and healthy.
  3. Regularly trim nails to avoid discomfort or injury.
  4. Gently clean your pet’s ears to prevent infections and wax buildup.
